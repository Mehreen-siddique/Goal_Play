# Goal_Play
Transform your daily life into an epic adventure!  Goal Play App gamifies your routine tasks and goals using Augmented Reality (AR) and RPG mechanics to boost motivation, productivity, and habit formation.The Goal Play is a cross-platform mobile application that addresses the critical challenge of maintaining motivation in personal development. With 92% of people abandoning their goals due to uninspiring traditional habit trackers, our solution transforms mundane daily activities into engaging quests with meaningful rewards, AR visualization, and social competition.
